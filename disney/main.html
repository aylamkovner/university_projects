What is Disneybounding?

At each of the Disney Parks properties, it's against the rules to dress in costume as a Disney character when you're over the age of 14. This is unfortunate, however, people have become created and come up with a solution, coined "disneybounding".

Disneybounding means dressing in the spirit of a (Disney) character. Typically the look is inspired via the colors chosen.

Want a random Disney movie to bound from? Check out the Disneybounding blog!
